# About

<div class="hero-panel">
  <p class="hero-lead">
    I build <strong>space AI systems</strong> that can perceive, reason, and act in on-orbit servicing scenarios.
  </p>
  <p>
    I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Application Technology at the
    <strong>University of Chinese Academy of Sciences</strong>, working at the
    <strong>Technology and Engineering Center for Space Utilization, Chinese Academy of Sciences</strong>.
    My work connects multimodal perception, tool-use agents, simulation, and hardware validation for space robotics.
  </p>
  <p>
    I care about research that closes the loop from <strong>algorithm design</strong> to
    <strong>system implementation</strong>. My recent projects focus on spacecraft perception,
    embodied intelligence, agentic control, and practical evaluation for deployable space systems.
  </p>
</div>
